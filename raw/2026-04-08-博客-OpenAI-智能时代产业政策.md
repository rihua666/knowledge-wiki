Ideas to keep people first.

As we move toward superintelligence, incremental policy updates won't be enough. OpenAI is offering a slate of people-first policy ideas designed to expand opportunity, share prosperity, and build resilient institutions.

To help sustain momentum, OpenAI is:
- welcoming feedback through newindustrialpolicy@openai.com
- establishing fellowships and research grants of up to $100,000 and up to $1M in API credits
- convening discussions at new OpenAI Workshop opening in May in Washington, DC
