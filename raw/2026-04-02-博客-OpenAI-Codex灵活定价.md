# Codex now offers pay-as-you-go pricing for teams
> 来源: https://openai.com/index/codex-flexible-pricing-for-teams/
> 抓取日期: 2026-04-09
> 标签: #openai #codex #pricing #enterprise

---

Starting today, teams on ChatGPT Business and Enterprise can add Codex-only seats to their workspaces with pay-as-you-go pricing, giving full access to Codex without a fixed seat fee. Codex-only seats have no rate limits, and usage is billed on token consumption.

ChatGPT Business annual price lowered from $25 to $20 per seat. New capabilities like Plugins and Automations make it easier to connect Codex to existing systems.

Key stats:
- 9 million paying business users rely on ChatGPT for work
- 2 million+ builders use Codex every week
- Codex users within Business/Enterprise grew 6x since January
- Customers: Notion, Ramp, Braintrust, Wasmer
